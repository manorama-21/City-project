Famous for.....


Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a city in Uttar Pradesh, India that's famous for being a pilgrimage center, a witness to important events in India's history, and its many monuments and attractions:

Pilgrimage center

Prayagraj is considered one of the holiest pilgrimage sites in India, and is the site of the Kumbh Mela, a massive gathering of pilgrims that takes place every six years.


History

Prayagraj was the center of the Indian freedom movement against British rule, and was the site of the beginning of Mahatma Gandhi's non-violent resistance movement.

Monuments and attractions

Prayagraj has many monuments and attractions, including Akbar's Fort, All Saints Cathedral, and Chandrashekhar Azad Park.

Food

Jalebi and rabri is a famous dessert in Prayagraj
